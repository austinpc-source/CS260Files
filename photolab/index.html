About the photographer

A photograph should do more than capture a particular time. Photos are there to capture an emotion or memory. You should look at one of my photos and feel as though you truly lived in that moment.

Growing up in some of the most scenic parts of Georgia, photography becomes essencial for showing the world a little slice of heaven. After moving to Utah, I started exploring the beautiful mountains in search of eye captivating backgrounds. It is my desire to use the environment to enhance to desired result of photos. Let me show you some of the best photos the best photos you will see in your lifetime.

Thank you for considering Clarke Photography for your next photo session. It brings me joy to spend time with clients during these memorable events. It is my goal to make this session as enjoyable and relaxing as possible. Let me take the stress off your shoulders so you can focus on what matters. If you have any questions or want to talk about your future session, feel free to contact me through the business facebook page.
