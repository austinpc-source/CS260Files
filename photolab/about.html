About Me

I grew up on a little island off the coast of Georgia. The nature is a very promenent part of the island and allows residents to appreciate natural beauty in all forms.

I have a wonderful family. Melissa and I have two kids. Daniela is three years old and Levi is one. Family is a big priority for me and this is why I like to capture people in moments of true expression. I like to savor the memories that come from being close to family.

I started taking photos professionally last week. My true inspiration came when I was asked to make a website for a class about how good of a photographer I am. I hope that I have convinced someone.
