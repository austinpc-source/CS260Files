Want to get a pet?

Alligators make wonderful pets. Have you ever watched a movie and wished for some company? Alligators are more than happy to give you a warm snuggle. Nothing feels better than the soft, warm comfort of alligator scales. These naturally lovable creatures will show you the affection you truly deserve.

What about their reputation?

Over the years, alligators have recieved a very misleading reputation for being cold blooded killers. Studies show that only 80% of all alligator pets attack their owners. This means that a delightful 20% of alligators are being misrepresented by the majority.

It is hard to understand why people are afraid of these evolved creatures. Over the course of thousands of years, the alligator has become a very efficient killer. Since when do we as a society shame individuals for excelling at an activity. We don't get mad at atheletes for being such overachievers and alligators should get the same treatment.
