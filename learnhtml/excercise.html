3 types of excercise

Balancing the muscles in the whole body is vital to living a happy life.
My favorite way to split up excercises is by the motion. See each section
to understand what muscles are being targeted.

Push

Push entails chest, shoulder, and tricep muscles. I like to start off with 3 sets
of barbell bench press. Inline, decline, and shoulder press are essencial to building
a well balanced upper chest. Lateral raises are perfect for building the shoulders.
Make sure to hit the triceps using tricep press and dips.

Pull

Pull entails back and biceps. Engage the back by starting with barbell rows.
Follow up with lat pulldowns and cable rows. The key to these exercises is to drive
the elbows back for full contraction of the back. The biceps react well to
bicep curls.

Legs

Legs may sound like an easy day because we only focus on one muscle group, but
it can also be one of the most difficult days. Start by doing 4 sets of barbell squats.
Follow this by single leg press or deadlifts. I recommend doing hamstring and quadricep
curls. Finishing with leg sleds will leave you barely able to walk.
