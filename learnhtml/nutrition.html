Nutrition

Eat to lift. Do not lift to eat. It is true that fitness is won an lost in the kitchen
I cannot lay out an exact plan that works universally for everybody, but there are some guidelines that
will make it easier.

Guidelines

  1. Calories are the best guide to indicate if you are eating too much or too little.
    • I recommend that you use an app like myfitnesspal to get a baseline of daily caloric intake.
    • Do not use this guide as an daily abosolute, but rather as an average for each day of the week.
  2. Decide what is the most important fitness goal. Are you focussed on weight loss, building muscle, or something like cholesterol?
    • Food is broken down into three major categories. The amount of: carbs, protein, and fat to consume will be affected
      by the overall goal.
  3. Make sustainable goals and be happy with the small progress that is being made.
